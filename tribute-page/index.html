Ayrton Senna

The greatest brazilian hero of all time

Here's a brief time line of Ayrton Senna's life

  • 1960: Born in São Paulo.
  • 1964: Started kart driving.
  • 1973: Started competing officialy in races.
  • 1977: Got his first racing victory.
  • 1981: Started competing in Europe. Got his first Formula Ford 1600 Championship the same year.
  • 1983: Got his first Formula 3 Championship racing for Dick Bennets team.
  • 1984: Started competing in Formula 1.
  • 1958-1987: He participated in 48 grand prix winning 6 of them.
  • 1988: Climbed up the podium for the first time as a McLaren-Honda pilot.
  • 1990: Became two-time world champion.
  • 1991: Became third-time world champion.
  • 1994: Got involved in a tragic accident that took his life.

Some interesting facts about Senna:

  • 161 Grand Prix
  • 65 Pole Positions
  • 41 Victories
  • 2982 Laps in the lead
  • 19 Fastest laps
  • 3x World Champion
